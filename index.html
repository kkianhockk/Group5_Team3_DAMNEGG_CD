Brand Story

2
Chef Elio Benedict transformed the humble egg into a culinary art form. With years of experience, he became known for his creative and sophisticated egg dishes. Before opening Damn Egg, Elio traveled the world, experimenting with all kinds of eggspoached, scrambled, baked, and raw. His unique approach quickly gained a loyal following.
In 2016, Elio opened Damn Egg, a fun and quirky restaurant known for dishes like the "Smokin' Sunny Side Volcano" and "Eggceptional Orbit". These playful, artistic creations earned rave reviews. By 2018, Damn Egg earned a 5-star Michelin rating, cementing its place in culinary history. Today, Elio continues to show the world that eggs are more than just food—they’re a revolution.
